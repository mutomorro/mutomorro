05 / Inline Links

Links within body text

When links sit inside a paragraph, they use accent purple with no underline at rest. On hover, an underline draws in from the left using a background-image technique so it wraps correctly across multiple lines of text without breaking. This is subtle but distinctive - the draw animation connects to the same language used by secondary buttons.

05b / Inline Links on Dark

On dark backgrounds, links shift to a lighter purple (#C9A4F0) for readability. The same underline draw behaviour applies. The accent colour adapts to its context while keeping the same interaction language.

06 / Image Treatments

Containing colour with editorial frames

Two treatments for handling colourful visuals within the restrained palette. Sharp corners on everything.

Gallery Mat

Warm padding (24px) around the image with thin border. Like a framed print in a gallery - the mat contains the artwork.

Editorial Offset Frame

Ghost border offset 8px behind and below. Adds depth and editorial sophistication without touching the image.

08 / Background Patterns

Three living patterns, one colour

All in accent purple (#9B51E0). Breathing gently. Each designed for a specific background colour. Seeded random numbers mean they render the same every time.

Soft Network

Drifting nodes with gentle connections

The most subtle pattern. Barely there but alive. For white sections where you want atmosphere without weight.

Woven Threads

Undulating threads weaving across

More texture than network. Feels like fabric or interconnected systems. For warm sections where you want depth.

Constellation

Twinkling connected points of light

The most atmospheric pattern. Stars drift, pulse, and connect. For dark sections where you want the living systems feeling at its strongest.

Design Principles

Two interaction languages

The diagonal fill motif runs through primary buttons, card footers, and navigation hovers. The underline draw runs through secondary buttons, inline links, footer links, and form focus states. Two languages, consistently applied.

Sharp corners everywhere

Border-radius: 0 on everything - buttons, cards, images, forms. This is the editorial line. No rounded corners. The system commits fully.

Hierarchy through weight, not colour

Headings and body are both black. Weight 400 headings against weight 300 body creates all the separation needed. Lead paragraphs in softer colour bridge the gap where needed.

Restraint is everything

Background patterns used sparingly. One marker highlight per heading. No animation on navigation or footer. Hero text stays static. The page unfolds quietly - it doesn't perform.
