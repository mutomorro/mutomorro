Where development will have most impact
Cross-team collaboration
High impact
Teams work well internally but struggle to collaborate across the organisation. This affects service quality and strategic delivery.
Affects: team (4.2), organisational (3.8)
People development capability
High impact
Managers lack the skills to develop their own teams. Development happens through courses, not through work. No coaching culture.
Affects: all levels (3.2 - 5.2)
Strategic thinking beyond leadership
High impact
Strategic capability concentrated in senior team. Teams cannot connect their work to wider organisational direction.
Affects: team (3.5), organisational (4.0)
