Change Approach Plan

Working with the system, not against it
Millbrook Academy Trust
Co-designed March 2026
Approach by stakeholder group
Senior leaders
Prioritise + protect
Need: confidence that change will be sequenced, not piled on
Reduce active changes from 7 to 3 priority areas. Senior leaders own sequencing decisions and protect staff from overload.
Middle leaders
Equip + empower
Need: authority and skills to lead change in their context
School-level adaptation authority. Practical change leadership toolkit. Regular peer support sessions with other middle leaders.
Teachers
Involve + respect
Need: voice in changes that affect their practice
Teacher design groups for each priority change. Pilot approach: test in volunteering schools first. Honest communication about what is changing and why.
Support staff
Include + inform
Need: to be included, not forgotten
Dedicated communication channel. Impact assessment for every change. Named contact for questions and concerns.
