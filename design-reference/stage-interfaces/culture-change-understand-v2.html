Emerging themes
Strong shared purpose across all levels
People clearly connect to the mission. Purpose scores are consistently high - this is a genuine foundation to build from.
Strength
Decisions sit too high in the organisation
Frontline and middle management report limited autonomy. Decisions escalate by default, creating bottlenecks and frustration.
Constraint
Collaboration exists but is not rewarded
Teams want to work together but recognition structures reinforce individual delivery over collective outcomes.
Pattern
Knowledge stays within teams
Expertise is hoarded, not shared. People solve the same problems in parallel because nothing connects the learning.
Constraint
Priority areas for action
01
Redistribute decision-making to where work happens

Impact
02
Create cross-team learning practices
Knowledge sharing scored lowest of all areas. Simple connection practices would have immediate effect
Impact
03
Align recognition with collaborative behaviour
Current incentives reinforce silos. Redesigning would unlock latent cross-team energy
