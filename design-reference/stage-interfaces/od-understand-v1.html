Emerging themes
Leadership commitment to development is genuine
Senior leaders consistently model learning behaviours and invest time in developing their teams. This is a real foundation.
Strength
Capability investment is reactive, not strategic
Development happens in response to problems rather than building ahead of need. No long-term capability plan exists across the organisation.
Constraint
Operational friction absorbs improvement energy
Teams spend so much time working around broken processes that they have no capacity for development work. Operations crowd out growth.
Constraint
Improvements happen in pockets, not across the system
Good practice exists in individual teams but does not spread. No mechanism connects local innovation to organisational learning.
