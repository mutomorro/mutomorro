Why Option B - the rationale
→
Directly addresses the core problem: work flows across practices, so the structure should too
→
Formalises what is already happening informally - builds on the real patterns, does not fight them
→
Frees partners from operational bottleneck to focus on strategy, client development and mentoring
→
Creates clear growth path for senior associates - authority matches capability
→
Practice expertise hubs protect deep specialism while enabling cross-functional delivery
→
Medium disruption is manageable - less risk than matrix, more impact than evolution
A structure designed around how work actually needs to happen
Option B was chosen because it addresses the core constraint - practice silos blocking cross-functional delivery - while preserving deep expertise. Designed by 64 people from across the firm, not imposed from the top.
