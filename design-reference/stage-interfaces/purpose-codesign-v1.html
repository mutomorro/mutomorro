Purpose integration touchpoints - where purpose needs to connect
Priority
Daily
decision-making 34
Decisions default to operational or financial logic. Purpose is rarely referenced. Teams lack a practical framework for purpose-aligned decisions.
Action agreed
Purpose decision framework - simple questions teams can use at any decision point
Priority
Performance
management 28
Nobody is measured on purpose alignment. What gets measured gets done. Purpose contribution is invisible in appraisals.
Action agreed
Integrate purpose contribution into performance conversations and recognition
Medium
Recruitment and
onboarding 58
Purpose features in job adverts but is not explored in interviews or embedded in induction. New starters feel a gap between promise and reality.
Action agreed
Redesign interview process and first-90-days experience around purpose connection
Medium
Service
design 51
Services evolved through operational logic. Some no longer clearly serve the mission. No systematic purpose check on service design decisions.
Action agreed
Purpose alignment review for all services, starting with the three most disconnected
Medium
Internal
communication 55
Purpose appears in big moments (annual reports, all-hands) but not in day-to-day communication. A special occasion, not a daily language.
Action agreed
Purpose storytelling practice - weekly examples of purpose in action shared across the organisation
Maintain
Strategy and
planning 76
Strategy clearly references purpose at board level. The gap is in translation to teams, not in strategic intent.
Action agreed
Strengthen strategy-to-team translation using the articulation framework
Purpose articulation - co-designed with 52 people
For the board
We exist to strengthen communities by creating opportunities that last beyond any single programme or intervention.
Strategic, long-term, systemic
For teams
Every piece of work we do should leave the community stronger than we found it - not just helped, but genuinely stronger.
Actionable, daily, grounded
For the people we serve
We are here to help you build something lasting - not just to solve today's problem, but to make tomorrow's easier.
Warm, empowering, honest
