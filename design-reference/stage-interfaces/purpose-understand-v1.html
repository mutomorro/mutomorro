Purpose connection map
Where purpose connects
Staff motivation
People joined because of the mission. That connection is genuine and deep - especially in frontline roles.
External reputation
The organisation is known for its purpose. Partners and funders cite it as a reason for engagement.
Strategic direction
The board and senior leadership genuinely use purpose as a compass for strategic choices.
Where purpose disconnects
Daily decision-making
Operational pressure overrides purpose. Teams make pragmatic choices that drift from the mission over time.
Performance management
Nobody is measured on purpose alignment. What gets measured gets done - and purpose is not measured.
Support functions
Finance, IT, and HR feel disconnected from purpose. They see themselves as enabling, not mission-driven.
