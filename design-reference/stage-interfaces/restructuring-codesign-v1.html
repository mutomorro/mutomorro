Communication commitments
No surprises
Everyone hears news directly, at the same time. No grapevine. No leaks. Honest and upfront.
Questions answered honestly
If we do not know the answer yet, we will say so. Regular Q&A sessions open to all staff.
Individual conversations first
Nobody finds out their future from a group email. High-impact staff get 1:1 conversations before wider announcements.
Weekly updates throughout
Short, honest updates every Friday. What has happened. What is coming. What people can expect.
