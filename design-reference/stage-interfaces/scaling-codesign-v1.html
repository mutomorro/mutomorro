Scaling Blueprint

Operational design for the next stage of growth
Greenfield Collective
Co-designed March 2026
Operational patterns redesigned for scale
Decision-making
New pattern
Currently: Everything routes through founders
Tiered decision rights. Teams own delivery decisions. Leads own resource decisions. Founders own strategic direction and partnerships only.
Team structure
New pattern
Currently: Flat, everyone connects to everyone
Three service-aligned teams (8-12 people each) with team leads. Cross-team coordination through a weekly leads forum, not through founders.
Communication
Evolve
Currently: Informal only, nothing documented
Team channels for daily work. Whole-org updates fortnightly. Documentation for decisions and processes - light, not bureaucratic.
Onboarding
Evolve
Currently: Shadow someone and figure it out
Structured first month with buddy system, team immersion, and purpose connection. Preserving the warmth while adding clarity.
