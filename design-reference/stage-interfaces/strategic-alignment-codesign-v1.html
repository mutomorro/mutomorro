Implementation sequence
Phase 1 - Months 1-3
Make the strategy visible and measurable
Redesign KPIs, launch quarterly alignment sessions, begin strategy translation programme. These create the foundation.
Phase 2 - Months 3-6
Realign how the organisation operates
Redesign operational rhythms, establish decision rights framework, begin budget rebalancing.
Depends on: Phase 1 KPIs in place
Phase 3 - Months 6-9
Embed and build internal capability
Alignment becomes self-monitoring. Teams run their own alignment checks. Strategy translation is continuous, not periodic.
Depends on: Phase 2 operational changes bedded in
Alignment principles agreed
Align the system, not just the people
Measure what the strategy needs, not what is easy
Translate, do not cascade
Build alignment as a capability, not a project
